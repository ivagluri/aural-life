Click / drag the grid to draw cells, or pick a stamp and click to place a pattern. Press play (or space). Pulse = each generation is a beat; sweep = a playhead scans columns (→/← reverses it, even mid-play). Repeating patterns (pulsar, blinker) become musical loops — a gun becomes an evolving stream. Try another rule (seeds crackles, no-death drones), rain to keep it alive forever, sym for kaleidoscope drawing, drift for lava-lamp colors, and zen for a full-window screensaver that reseeds itself. Keys: space=play · s=step · c=clear · r=random · z=zen.
